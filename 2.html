Biography

I’m an Associate Research Scientist at Columbia University Medical Center. My research investigates the regulation of hematopoietic stem cells (HSCs) under physiological and regenerative conditions using genetic, molecular, and bioinformatics approaches. I am particularly interested in mechanisms such as epitranscriptomic and splicing regulation that drive HSC function and self-renewal.
